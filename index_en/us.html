About us

The bearsbox workshop was founded in 2016.

Since creating toy boxes for our own use in 2014, we have grown before the commercial production of children's furniture for storing toys and not only.

The main idea when opening the workshop was to create a high-quality, convenient and environmentally friendly clean furniture. We found that buy normal, children's toy furniture made not made of plastic or chipboard, potchi impossible. Then we had the idea of creating our own workshop's. We decided that we will not save on materials and coatings, while sacrificing quality and safety. That's why, toy boxes, shelving, shelves, and pencil cases, hand-made by our craftsmen from eco-friendly birch plywood (class E1), painted with a highly ecological water-based wax varnish.
